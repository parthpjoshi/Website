About Us

Surana Architects is a multidisciplinary design firm committed to shaping spaces that are functional, expressive, and timeless. With a passion for innovation and an eye for detail, we offer comprehensive services in Architecture, Interior Design, Graphic Design, MEP (Mechanical, Electrical & Plumbing) Services, and 3D Visualization.

At Surana Architects, we believe that every project is an opportunity to tell a story—your story. Whether it's crafting efficient layouts, curating inspiring interiors, designing impactful visuals, or developing accurate technical systems, we ensure that every element aligns with your vision.

Our team combines creativity with technical expertise to deliver cohesive and sustainable design solutions tailored to the needs of clients across residential, commercial, and institutional sectors.

From concept to execution, Surana Architects is your trusted partner in turning ideas into built reality.

Architectural Planning & Design Features

Key considerations we work on for every project

Functionality & Space Utilization

  • Smart zoning (public, private, semi-private)
  • Logical circulation and flow
  • Multi-functional areas
  • Optimized use of plot area, orientation, and height

Contextual & Climate-Responsive Design

  • Building aligned with site, sun path, and wind
  • Natural light and ventilation
  • Rainwater harvesting, shading devices
  • Locally sourced materials where possible

Sustainability & Energy Efficiency

  • Use of passive solar design
  • Thermal comfort via insulation, green roofs, etc.
  • Provision for solar panels, STPs, and eco-tech

Structural Safety & Services Integration

  • Compliance with structural safety norms
  • Integration of MEP (Mechanical, Electrical, Plumbing)

Flexibility & Future-Proofing

  • Expandable floor plans
  • Provisions for vertical expansion
  • Modular structural grid for adaptation
