Architectural Planning & Design Features

Key considerations we work on for every project

Functionality & Space Utilization

  • Smart zoning (public, private, semi-private)
  • Logical circulation and flow
  • Multi-functional areas
  • Optimized use of plot area, orientation, and height

Contextual & Climate-Responsive Design

  • Building aligned with site, sun path, and wind
  • Natural light and ventilation
  • Rainwater harvesting, shading devices
  • Locally sourced materials where possible

Sustainability & Energy Efficiency

  • Use of passive solar design
  • Thermal comfort via insulation, green roofs, etc.
  • Provision for solar panels, STPs, and eco-tech

Structural Safety & Services Integration

  • Compliance with structural safety norms
  • Integration of MEP (Mechanical, Electrical, Plumbing)

Flexibility & Future-Proofing

  • Expandable floor plans
  • Provisions for vertical expansion
  • Modular structural grid for adaptation
